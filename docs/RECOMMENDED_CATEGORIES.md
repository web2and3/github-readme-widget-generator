# Recommended Product Categories

Ideas for expanding the app beyond the current **GitHub Streak Card**, **Skill Set Widget**, and **Followers Check**.

---

## 1. Network & relationship management

- **Followers overview** (current) – Unfollowers, Not Mutuals, Followers, Following, Whitelist.
- **Engagement tracking** – Who interacts most with your repos; who you interact with most.
- **Network growth** – Follower/following trends over time.
- **Suggested connections** – Based on shared interests (e.g. common starred repos, followed users, languages).

---

## 2. Contribution & activity insights

- **Personal contribution stats** (current: Streak, Skill-set) – Commits, issues, PRs, code reviews.
- **Repository activity** – Contributions to specific repos (yours or others’).
- **Language & tech stack** – Breakdown of languages used, skill visualization.
- **Activity heatmaps** – Contribution patterns over weeks/months/years.

---

## 3. Project & repository management

- **Repository overview card** – Key stats for a repo (stars, forks, open issues, PRs, last commit, license).
- **Starred repo manager** – Organize, search, or categorize starred repos.
- **Issue & PR tracking** – Summaries of open/closed issues and PRs across owned or watched repos.

---

## 4. Community & collaboration

- **Organization/team insights** – Metrics on team contributions and collaboration (if applicable).
- **Top contributors** – Highlight key contributors to your projects.

---

## 5. Personalization & customization

- **Custom widget builder** – Build custom visualizations from GitHub metrics.
- **Theme customizer** – Color palette and layout options beyond light/dark.

---

*Use these as a roadmap for new widgets, pages, or sections.*
